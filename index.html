What Is It?

Pokémon Crystal added a lot of new features to the already great Silver and Gold versions. However one feature that didn't change so nicely was resetting the in-game clock. What was normally an easy to accomplish thing now required players to due some pretty obscure mathematics to obtain a 'Password'. To do this, players would have to cross reference a chart to convert the characters into numbers, calculate remainders of Trainer IDs and wallet amounts from some foreign value, and do some benign addition and subtraction to obtain the final result. These calculations wouldn't work with scientific calculators so you would have to due all the division by hand. This tool aims at simplifying this whole process, it just requires the player's name, trainer id, and wallet amount to due all the boring calculations for you.

How To Use It?

  1. Get into your Pokémon Crystal save file. Platform doesn't matter (GBC, Emulator, VC).
  2. Open up the start menu, and navigate to your Trainer Card.
  3. Take note of your Trainer Name (capitalization matters).
  4. Take note of your Trainer ID.
  5. Take note of your current amount of Cash (don't worry about your savings with your mom).
  6. Save and exit!
  7. Boot up the game again.
  8. At the title screen, hold DOWN, SELECT, B.
  9. While still holding SELECT, let go of DOWN and B.
  10. Press and hold UP and LEFT.
  11. Let go of SELECT.
  12. This might take a couple tries, just keep trying.
  13. If you succeed, it'll ask you if you want to change the time. Select 'YES'.
  14. Provide the form above your Trainer Name, Trainer ID, and Money.
  15. Click 'Generate Password', enter the result into your game.
